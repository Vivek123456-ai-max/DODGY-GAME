Enter the chaotic world of Dodgy, a fast-paced arcade survival game where your only mission is to stay alive. Dodge deadly obstacles, survive unpredictable challenges, and test your reflexes in an endless battle for the highest score. The longer you survive, the harder it gets. Simple controls, intense gameplay, and addictive action make Dodgy perfect for quick gaming sessions and competitive leaderboard battles.
🎮 Features:
Endless dodge-based gameplay
Increasing difficulty levels
Smooth and responsive controls
Minimalist and stylish graphics
High-score challenge system
Fun, addictive, and easy to play
